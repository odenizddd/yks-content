İlkbin — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

İlkbin is a study app for students preparing for the YKS exam. It shows bite-sized study cards that you swipe through. We built it to be private by design: İlkbin does not require an account and does not collect personal information about you.

The short version. No sign-up. No ads. No analytics or tracking SDKs. We don't build a profile of you, and we don't sell or share your data, because we don't collect it in the first place.

1. Who we are

İlkbin (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is published by Odd Labs. If you have any questions about this policy or your privacy, contact us at coolstuff447@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect personal data. The app has no user accounts, no login, and no registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information, and the app contains no advertising or third-party analytics or tracking software.

3. Information stored on your device

To work offline and stay up to date, the app stores study content locally on your device:

  • Study content cache. The most recently downloaded set of study cards is saved in the app's private storage so the feed works without a network connection.
  • App preferences. Lightweight settings such as your selected exam track and subject filter may be kept on the device.

This information stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

4. Network requests

The app periodically checks for updated study content by downloading static files over a secure (HTTPS) connection from our content host (a static file-hosting service). No personal data is sent in these requests — the app only asks for the latest content files.

As with any request to any website, our hosting provider may automatically record standard technical information (such as your IP address, device/browser type, and the time of the request) in its server logs as part of delivering the files. We do not use this information to identify you, and we do not combine it with any other data. It is handled by the hosting provider under its own policies.

5. Permissions

The app only uses internet access, which is required to download updated study content. It does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or other sensitive device features.

6. How we use information

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not use it for profiling, advertising, marketing, or any automated decision-making. The locally stored content described in Section 3 is used solely to display the study feed on your device.

7. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, and we have no third-party advertising or analytics partners in the app. The only third party involved is the static content host that delivers the study files, as described in Section 4.

8. Children's privacy

İlkbin is intended for students preparing for the YKS exam, some of whom may be minors. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has somehow provided us personal information, please contact us and we will address it; in practice the app provides no way to submit personal information to us.

9. Data security

Content is downloaded over encrypted HTTPS connections, and the on-device cache is stored in the app's private storage area provided by your operating system. Because we hold no personal data on any server, there is no personal data of yours for us to lose.

10. Your rights

You remain in control of the data stored on your device: you can clear the app's cached content at any time through your device settings, or by uninstalling the app. As we do not hold personal data about you, there is nothing further for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy? Reach us at coolstuff447@gmail.com.
